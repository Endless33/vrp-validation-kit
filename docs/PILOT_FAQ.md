Pilot FAQ

Is the pilot a production deployment?

No.

The purpose of the pilot is technical validation.

---

Does the pilot require replacing existing infrastructure?

No.

The objective is evaluation and validation.

---

Does the pilot require source code access?

Not necessarily.

Pilot scope depends on validation objectives.

---

What is being evaluated?

Examples include:

- Replay containment
- Session continuity
- Recovery behavior
- Authority transitions
- Canonical history preservation

---

What evidence is produced?

Typical artifacts include:

- Runtime logs
- Validation verdicts
- Failure observations
- Validation reports

---

How long does a pilot usually take?

Typical duration:

2–4 weeks

---

What happens after a successful pilot?

Possible outcomes include:

- Additional validation
- Extended evaluation
- Integration discussions
- Architecture review
