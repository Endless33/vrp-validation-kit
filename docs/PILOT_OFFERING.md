VRP Pilot Offering

Objective

The purpose of a VRP pilot is to evaluate execution correctness under real operational conditions.

The objective is not production deployment.

The objective is technical validation.

---

What Is Evaluated

Potential evaluation areas include:

- Replay containment
- Session continuity
- Authority transitions
- Epoch progression
- Runtime recovery
- Transport migration
- Canonical history preservation

---

Typical Pilot Duration

2–4 weeks

---

Pilot Deliverables

Example deliverables:

- Validation report
- Runtime evidence
- Failure analysis
- Reproduction steps
- Technical recommendations

---

Pilot Success Criteria

A pilot is considered successful when:

- Validation scenarios execute successfully
- Expected verdicts are observed
- Invariants remain preserved
- Results are reproducible

---

Intended Audience

Examples include:

- Infrastructure teams
- Networking teams
- Distributed systems teams
- Edge computing teams
- Robotics platforms
- Reliability-focused environments
