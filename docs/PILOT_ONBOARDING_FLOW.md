Pilot Onboarding Flow

Step 1

Initial technical discussion.

Objective:

Understand environment constraints.

---

Step 2

Architecture review.

Topics may include:

- Session model
- Failure model
- Recovery model
- Validation objectives

---

Step 3

Validation planning.

Questions:

Which failures matter most?

What would constitute success?

What evidence is required?

---

Step 4

Scenario execution.

Examples:

- Replay validation
- Recovery validation
- Transport migration validation
- Authority transition validation

---

Step 5

Evidence collection.

Artifacts:

- Runtime logs
- Verdicts
- Failure observations
- Reproduction steps

---

Step 6

Pilot review.

Outputs:

- Findings
- Recommendations
- Potential next steps
